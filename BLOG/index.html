Exploring Paris

Paris, the city of lights, is known for its art,fashion, and culture. Highlight include the Eiffel Tower ,Louvre Museum, and charming cafes.

  • Best time to visit: April to June, October to early November
  • Must-try food: Croissants, Escarget, Macarons
  • Popular activities : Seine River Cruise, visit Montmartre, Explore le marais

Exploring Tokyo

Tokyo, the city of Towers, is known for its huge Towers,fashion, and culture. Highlight include the Eiffel Tower ,Louvre Museum, and charming cafes.

  • Best time to visit: july to september, November to early January
  • Must-try food: Momos,Chowmein,Kuski
  • Popular activities : Bungee jumping,River rafting, Bloging
